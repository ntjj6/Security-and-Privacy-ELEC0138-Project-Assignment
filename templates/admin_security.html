Attack chain summary

Use these stages in the report and demo narration
1
Reconnaissance
Repeated username failures reveal valid accounts.
2
Credential attack
Password guessing continues until a successful login is recorded.
3
Fraud and abuse
Attackers transfer to risky recipients or submit repeated transfer requests.
4
Availability pressure
High request bursts and slower responses show the DDoS baseline.
